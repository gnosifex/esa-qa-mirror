# EBA Q&A 2013_161

## Question

Template 6 - Breakdown of loans and advances to non-financial corporations by NACE codes and by residence of the counterparty. Should this table be delivered per country?

## Background

In previous draft version there was a field a cell z-axis for Total country of residence of the counterpart y and this version that cell is removed. It is misleading as in the tile is still written 'by residence of the counterpart y'

## Answer

F 06.00 shall be reported only on total loans and advances without geographical breakdown. The country-by-country breakdown shall be reported in template F 20.07 as per Article 9(2)(d) of the Regulation (EU) No 680/2014 13 ITS on supervisory reporting of institutionsDraft ITS on Supervisory reporting.

As a result, the references to the ITS were updated and the disclaimer deleted. For reasons of transparency, revisions are highlighted in track changes.
