# EBA Q&A 2014_1024

## Question

The header for column 125 in Template C.09.02 reads: RISK WEIGHTED EXPOSURE AMOUNT PRE AFTER SME-SUPPORTING FACTOR The correct heading is: RISK WEIGHTED EXPOSURE AMOUNT AFTER SME-SUPPORTING FACTOR

## Background

Review of Templates

## Answer

In the Data Point Model V2.0.0, the header of column 125 in template C 09.02 of Regulation (EU) No 680/2014 13 ITS on Supervisory Reporting of institutions (ITS) should read RISK WEIGHTED EXPOSURE AMOUNT AFTER SME-SUPPORTING FACTOR. The header will be amended accordingly.
