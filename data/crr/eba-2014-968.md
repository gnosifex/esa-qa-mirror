# EBA Q&A 2014_968

## Question

Could you please advise what risk weight/treatments should be applied to International Organisations that are not included in Article 118 of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 (CRR)?

## Background

CRR unclear.

## Answer

Exposures to international organisations not included in the exhaustive list of Article 118 of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 (CRR) shall be treated as exposures to corporates (Article 122). In case of intergovernmental organisations where the participating sovereigns provide guarantees these can be taken into account conform the requirements laid down in Part Three Chapter 4 ('Credit Risk mitigation') of the CRR.
