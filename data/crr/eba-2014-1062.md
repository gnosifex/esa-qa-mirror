# EBA Q&A 2014_1062

## Question

With reference to the definition of "Retail Deposits", does the EUR1 million limit apply to 'natural persons'? Or does it solely apply to SMEs and companies eligible for the treatments set out in Article 153(4) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 (CRR)?

## Background

This is the wording found in Article 411 (2): “'retail deposit' means a liability to a natural person or to an SME, where the natural person or the SME would qualify for the retail exposure class under the Standardised or IRB approaches for credit risk, or a liability to a company which is eligible for the treatment set out in Article 153(4)2 and where the aggregate deposits by all such enterprises on a group basis do not exceed EUR 1 million.”

## Answer

Article 411(2) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 is to be interpreted such that the ceiling on aggregate deposits mentioned therein does not apply to natural persons.
