# EBA Q&A 2013_120

## Question

What amount should be reported on row 260 of template F1.3, the dividends distributed during the year or the proposed dividends for the financial year?

## Background

It is unclear what amount should be reported.

## Answer

The amount to be reported in row 260 of template F 01.03 is the dividends distributed during the financial year and not the proposed dividends for the financial year .
