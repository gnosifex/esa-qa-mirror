# EBA Q&A 2022_6615

## Question

In ESG disclosure template 5:

Should the amounts disclosed in column b be equal to the carrying amounts disclosed in column a in template 1, or;

Should the amounts in column b only include exposures exposed to physical risk according to the definition in the new Article 18a § 1c?

## Background

The instructions to the disclosure templates do not give any guidance to what should be included in column b, Template 5, gross carrying amount.

## Answer

The total sum across all geographical areas of the amounts to be disclosed in Column b of Template 5 of Annex 1 to Implementing Regulation (EU) 2022/2453 should be identical to the final sum of the amount disclosed in column a of Template 1 and not be restricted to the part of the institution’s gross carrying amount exposed to physical risks.
