# EBA Q&A 2014_1466

## Question

Validation rule v0668_m states that r{r010} = sum(r020-100). We believe this validation rule will fail due to the fact that the categorisation of row 100 (x3 - 'Defaulted') for dimension Impairment status is different than the one for row 010 (x5 -'Non defaulted') which represents the total as per the validation rule.

## Background

Inconsistency between validation rules and DPM categorisation.

## Answer

The DPM will be amended with regard to the data points included in template C 41.00 of Annex X of Regulation (EU) No 680/2014. However, v0668_m will remain active, as no issues were observed in actual reports.
