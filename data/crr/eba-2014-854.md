# EBA Q&A 2014_854

## Question

Ilogical validations in Annex XV

## Background

There are several validations that do not appear to work given the contents of the cells referenced. Can you please advise as to the appropriate treatment for these items? Please see attached a file which includes specific validations which we believe may be illogical or contain errors.

## Answer

In case a validation rule is not correct, Annex XV of Regulation (EU) No 680/2014 - ITS on Supervisory Reporting of institutions will be amended accordingly. More Details are included in the attached file.
