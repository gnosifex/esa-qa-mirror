# EBA Q&A 2020_5646

## Question

As clarified in 2019_4818 that withdrawable central bank reserve may be reported as CB eligible in C71. The same CB reserve is reported in C66 row 3.2 also as CB eligible. In C66 CB eligible HQLA are reported in memo line 13. C66 instruction has specifically mentioned to report row 3.3, 3.4 and 3.5 CB eligible values in memo line 13. As we have now agreed to report CB withdrawable reserve as CB eligible for C71 should this also be included in memo line 13 of C66 reporting.

## Background

Revert of 2019_4818 "In accordance with paragraph 6 of Annex XXI of amended Regulation (EU) No 680/2014 and as confirmed in EBA Q&A 4217, institutions shall include under row 120 of supervisory reporting template C 71.00, concentrations of counterbalancing capacity with a central bank as counterparty, which also includes central bank deposits / reserves. Provided that institutions are permitted to withdraw such deposits / reserves at any time during stress periods, the full amount of such deposits / reserves may be reported under column 090 of this template (‘Collateral value CB-eligible’).

## Answer

In accordance with Annex XXIII of amended Regulation (EU) No 680/2014 (ITS on Supervisory Reporting), for the purpose of completing the C 66.01 maturity ladder template referred to in Annex XXII of the same regulation, institutions shall report under row ID 13 (row 1230) of the C 66.01 maturity ladder template the amount reported in IDs 3.3, 3.4 and 3.5 which is eligible collateral for standard liquidity operations of the central bank to which the institution has direct access at its level of consolidation. Since no reference is made to withdrawable central bank reserves reported under ID 3.2 (Row 740), they shall not be included under ID 13 (row 1230).
