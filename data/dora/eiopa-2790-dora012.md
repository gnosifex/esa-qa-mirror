# EIOPA Q&A 2790 - DORA012

## Question

Can competent authorities in DORA publish decisions relating to administrative penalties and remedial measures, or can they only publish decisions on administrative penalties?

## Answer

The answer to this question is provided by the European Commission .

As a matter of principle, the publication of competent authorities’ decisions is an act of transparency and accountability towards their market participants and to consumers/investors. Article 54 should not be read as limiting the power of competent authorities to only publishing decisions on penalties. Competent authorities in any case are administrative bodies, and may be subject to national administrative law (in addition to the Union acquis) which may allow for the publication of all their decisions.
