# EBA Q&A 2013_411

## Question

Does the requirement to publish the administrative penalties (Article 68 of Directive 2013/36/EU (CRD)) include publishing supervisory powers listed in Article 104?

## Background

Administrative penalties and supervisory powers are not defined in the CRD.

## Answer

Article 68 of Directive 2013/36/EU (CRD) covers only the publications of administrative penalties. It does not require the disclosure of measures taken by the competent authority within the remit of supervisory powers as defined by Article 104 of CRD.
