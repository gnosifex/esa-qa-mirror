# EBA Q&A 2015_2503

## Question

Should the template 105.01 be filled also for LDPs?

## Background

For specific type of LDPs the information required in template 105 are not available/significant.

## Answer

The templates C 105.01, C 105.02 and C 105.03 of Annex III of Draft ITS on Supervisory Reporting for Institutions for benchmarking the internal approaches (ITS on benchmarking) have to be submitted for the portfolios requested in c010 of template C105.02 of Annex IV and in accordance with the provisions of Art. 7 of the Draft ITS on benchmarking. It should be noted that EL-models are excluded from reporting requirements in the templates 105.
